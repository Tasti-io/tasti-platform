01 — Scope of Work

Here's exactly what's included in your monthly plan:

  • Content creation — photos, short videos, and captions tailored to your brand and cuisine
  • Post scheduling — consistent, well-timed publishing to maximise reach and engagement
  • Instagram strategy — hashtag research, bio optimisation, and content calendar planning
  • Stories management — behind-the-scenes moments, promos, and interactive elements
  • Community engagement — responses to comments and DMs

Anything outside this scope — photography shoots on-site, full account rebrand — can be discussed separately.

02 — Payment

  • Monthly subscription — billed at the start of each cycle
  • Payment is due upfront before work begins for that month
  • No refunds once the billing cycle has started — if you cancel mid-month, service continues through the end of the paid period
  • Accepted payment methods: card via Stripe

03 — Term & Cancellation

  • This is a month-to-month agreement — no long-term lock-in
  • Either party can cancel by giving 7 days' written notice before the next billing date
  • Notice can be sent via email to hello@tasti.io
  • After cancellation, all content created during the engagement remains yours to keep

04 — Your Responsibilities

To help us do our best work, we ask that you:

  • Grant us access to your Instagram account and/or Meta Business Suite (Editor role is preferred)
  • Provide any brand assets — logo, photos, videos, menu — in a timely manner
  • Review and approve content within the agreed timeframe (typically 48 hours); delays may affect the posting schedule
  • Keep us in the loop about upcoming events, specials, or changes to your menu

05 — Content & Approvals

  • By default, we'll send content for your review before publishing
  • If you prefer auto-publishing (pre-approved content goes live without your sign-off each time), we're happy to work that way — just let us know
  • You retain ownership of all content we create for your brand
  • We may use anonymised examples of our work in our portfolio unless you ask us not to

06 — A Note on Liability

We take our work seriously and always aim for great results. That said, a few things are outside our control:

  • We're not responsible for changes to Instagram's algorithm, platform outages, or account restrictions imposed by Meta
  • We can't guarantee specific follower counts or engagement numbers — social media results depend on many factors
  • We're not liable for any indirect losses related to your social media performance

If something ever goes wrong on our end, we'll always communicate openly and work to make it right.
